## The Harambee Summer Festival

The **Harambee Summer Festival** is a week-long celebration of Black culture and community, bringing together families with children of African heritage for a time of connection, learning, and fun.  

Open to **biological, adoptive, and foster families**, the Festival features workshops for parents, cultural programming for youth, and shared family activities designed to strengthen bonds and build lifelong memories.
